# Stock Analysis Template

## CSV Thesis Formula (copy this into your thesis cell)
`[Driver] + [Moat]; validate: [KPI1/KPI2]; risk: [Main risk]`

Example:
`Cloud + AI enterprise adoption; validate: Azure growth & margin; risk: enterprise IT budget cut`

## 1) Company Background
- What does the company sell?
- Who are its customers?
- Main competitive moat?

## 2) Fundamental Snapshot
- Revenue growth trend (YoY)
- Profitability quality (gross/operating margins)
- Balance sheet risk (debt burden)

## 3) News & Sentiment (last 7 days)
- Top 3 positive signals
- Top 3 risk signals
- Net sentiment score
